A Short Tale of Two Sisters

Where to Adopt:

Persephone aka Percy

Adopted: August 29, 2009

It had been a while since I had any pets. A friend of mine had found a kindle of seven kittens who had been abandoned by their mother. He asked me if I was interested in adopting one. My place seemed pretty empty and lonely without a furry companion so I decided to adopt two of them: two black kittens so they can keep each other company while I was away. I named the two kittens, Persephone (Percy for short) and Artemis, after Greek deities.

Percy and Artemis got in quite a lot of mischief throughout the house: breaking things, knocking things over while wrestling, and climbing onto tops of shelves. They enjoyed each others' company and played for hours on end. Unfortunately, after 8 years, Artemis passed away and left Percy and me by ourselves. Three years would pass and I noticed Percy's loneliness increasing while I was away. So I made the decision to adopt another cat.

Freya

Adopted:Sep 19, 2019

I felt like it was time for Percy to have another furry companion. It was difficult to decide which cat to adopt after visiting several animal shelters in the area. It was at the Seattle Humane Society in Bellevue, WA where I saw this tiny, black kitten, alone in the plexiglass cage. The kitten had these humongous ears and I was instantly smitten with this kitten. I named her Freya after the Norse goddess of love.

I took the gradual steps to introduce the new kitten to Percy over a span of two and a half weeks. Eventually, Percy got to meet Freya and Percy was definitely curious. I was worried that the difference in their age would be problematic. There was this high energy, little ball of fur who was running around, up and down the stairs, and exploring her new home. Freya wanted to engage in high energy and rough play with Percy, who is 10 years her senior, but Percy would not partake in Freya's playfulness. So I took the task to play with Freya to expend most of her kitten energy. It's enabled Freya and Percy to have a better relationship and enjoy each other's company.
