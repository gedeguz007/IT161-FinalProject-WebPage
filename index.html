A Tale of Two Black Cats

Where to Adopt:

Seattle Humane Society Seattle Animal Shelter Emerald City Kitty Harbor

Persephone aka Percy

Adopted: August 29, 2009

I adopted two cute black kittens two years after my 7 year old tortie, short-legged cat, Sazerac, passed away in April 2007. My place seemed pretty empty and lonely without a furry companion for those two years. I named the two kittens, Persephone and Artemis.

After 8 years, Artemis passed away and left Percy and me by ourselves. It would take 3 years before I would adopt another kitten.

Freya

Adopted:Sep 19, 2019

Freya was adopted from the Seattle Humane Society in Bellevue, WA. I felt like it was time for Percy to have another furry companion. I was debating on adopting an older cat closer to Percy's age or a kitten. In the end, I decided on a kitten.

I took the gradual steps to introduce the new kitten to Percy over a span of two and a half weeks. Eventually Percy got to meet Freya and Percy was definitely curious. There was this high energy, little ball of fur who was running around, up and down the stairs, exploring her new home. As Freya matured, she wanted to engage in high energy and rough play with Percy, who is 10 years her senior. Percy did not enjoy this much so I took the task to play with Freya to expend most of her kitten energy. It's enabled Freya and Percy to have a better relationship and enjoy each other's company.
